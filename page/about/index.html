About Shawn M. O'Hare

I am a mathematician, data scientist, and skeptic. Broadly, I am interested in the applications of mathematics—specifically abstract algebra—to machine learning, statistics, and artificial intelligence.

The writings on this site represent an eclectic mixture of my interests. Generally the articles aim to please a mathematically sophisticated audience while still being informative to a lay reader.
